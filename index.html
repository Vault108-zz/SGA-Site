About Us

Student Government is here to voice your concerns. Besides serving on many College and statewide committees, officers represent the student body to College policy makers; the President attends monthly Board of Trustee meetings and the Vice President represents students to the College Council at bi-monthly meetings. In their other role, Student Government also assists the Activities Director in planning and providing programs and activities. So make it a point to get to know your student officers. Come to Student Government meetings as often as you can. Meetings are Tuesdays at 2:30 pm, Caroline 216. Think about serving on Student Government.
